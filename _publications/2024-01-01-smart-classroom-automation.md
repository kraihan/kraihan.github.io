This paper presents an **AI-based smart classroom automation system** that integrates **voice, gesture, and face recognition** to streamline attendance and interaction. The system leverages computer vision and speech processing to recognize students and interpret instructor commands.

The prototype demonstrates improved efficiency and reduced manual overhead in classroom management, illustrating how multi-modal AI can support educational environments.
